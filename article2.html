Ex-NBA allstar comes to IRBL to coach the San Antonio Spurs

Por Pepe Pino 04 Abr, 2018 / 15:10

Zazag37 was in the news for all the wrong reasons the past couple weeks, as the former NRBA player took shots at seemingly everyone he ever worked with in his upcoming book. And after the juiciest tidbits leaked, Zaza looked upon the scorched earth at his feet and decided, "Yes, now is the time to voice the fact that I would like a coaching job, please." We have some good news for Zaza: After all this, he's probably going to get that chance. IRBL coaching gigs are a precious commodity, and franchises are looking to first-timers from the assistant and college ranks with increasing regularity. That's probably the right decision; fresh perspectives often lead to success (with some glaring failures among those coaching rookies, to be sure).
